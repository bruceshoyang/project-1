Our Training Services

ShoFit offers a variety of training options designed to meet you where you are—whether you’re new to the gym or chasing your next personal record.

Strength Training

Progressive barbell and dumbbell programs focused on building muscle, power, and overall strength.

HIIT & Conditioning

High-intensity interval classes that improve endurance and burn calories in under 45 minutes.

Personal Training

One-on-one coaching tailored to your goals, injury history, and experience level.

Small Group Classes

Train with a small group and a coach who can still correct form and keep you accountable.

Mobility & Recovery

Stretching and mobility sessions focused on reducing stiffness and improving range of motion.

Nutrition Guidance

Basic nutrition coaching to support fat loss, muscle gain, and overall performance in the gym.
